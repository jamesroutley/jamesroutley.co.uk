Mind Your Head

Mind Your Head is an Oxford based organisation aiming to increase awareness about mental health issues. I was asked to rebuild their website and blog, which was previously a poorly designed Wordpress site. Due to the large amount of information which needed to be presented by the site, I decided on a multi-page website, and used Tumblr for the blog, for its ease of use.
